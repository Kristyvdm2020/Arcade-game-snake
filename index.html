Please enter your name and press play
    Rules of the game:
  • The point of the game is to earn as many points as you can.
  • You lose the game if the snake runs into a wall or the head overlaps the body.
  • To control the direction of the snake, use the arrow keys.
  • All apples earn 1 point.
  • Blue apples slow down the snake.
  • Green apples swap the controls of the snake.
  • Gold apples speed up the snake.
